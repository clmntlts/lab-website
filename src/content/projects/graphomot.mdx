The GraphoMot project investigates the graphomotor aspects of handwriting in typically developing children and children with dyslexia. Using behavioural kinematics, neuroimaging (fMRI), and intervention studies, the project examines how handwriting automaticity develops and where it breaks down in developmental reading disorders.

Key questions include how handwriting speed and quality co-develop with spelling and reading proficiency, whether graphomotor difficulties in dyslexia are a cause or a consequence of poor literacy, and whether targeted handwriting interventions can benefit orthographic learning.
