Thomas Brun is a second-year PhD student investigating predictive coding mechanisms in the visual hierarchy. His research aims to test normative theories of neural computation against large-scale electrophysiology data from macaque visual cortex.

He completed his MSc in Cognitive Neuroscience at ENS Paris (2022) and his BSc in Physics at Sorbonne Université (2020). During his master's he interned at DeepMind where he worked on biologically plausible learning algorithms.
