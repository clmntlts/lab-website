Caroline Vander Stappen completed her PhD at UCLouvain in 2019, examining the contributions of phonological awareness and rapid automatized naming to reading and spelling development. Her dissertation investigated the nature of these two phonological competencies and their independent roles in literacy acquisition. She also conducted a randomised controlled trial on RAN training in children with dyslexia.
