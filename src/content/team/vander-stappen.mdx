Charlotte Vander Stappen completed her PhD at UCLouvain in 2019, investigating phonological awareness, rapid automatized naming (RAN), and their role in predicting reading outcomes. Her doctoral dissertation examined RAN-based training interventions in children with dyslexia, combining behavioural and neuroimaging data.
