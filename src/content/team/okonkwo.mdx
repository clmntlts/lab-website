Aisha Okonkwo is a third-year PhD student in the Neural Computation Laboratory. She is developing generative models of neural population activity in motor cortex, with a focus on variational methods that respect the geometry of neural manifolds.

She holds an MSc in Machine Learning from UCL (2021) and a BSc in Electrical Engineering from the University of Lagos (2019). Her MSc thesis examined disentangled representations in deep generative models for biomedical time series.

Aisha is a Graduate School Fellow and recipient of the Horizon Europe Doctoral Fellowship (2021).
