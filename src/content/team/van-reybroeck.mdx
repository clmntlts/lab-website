Professor at UCLouvain's Faculty of Psychology and Educational Sciences, Marie Van Reybroeck founded and leads the DeLi Lab. She teaches written language acquisition and disorders in master's programmes for speech therapy and educational sciences. Her research focuses on the cognitive processes underlying reading and spelling in typically developing children and those with dyslexia, with a particular interest in intervention efficacy in school and therapeutic contexts.
