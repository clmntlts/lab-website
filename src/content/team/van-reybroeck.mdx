Marie Van Reybroeck is Professor of Developmental Psychology at the Université catholique de Louvain (UCLouvain), where she leads the Development of Literacy (DeLi) Lab. Her research focuses on the acquisition of written language — reading, spelling, and handwriting — in typically developing children and those with developmental disorders such as dyslexia and dysorthography.

She completed her PhD at UCLouvain and has since built an internationally recognised research programme on literacy prerequisites (phonological awareness, rapid automatized naming), grammatical spelling, and the graphomotor aspects of handwriting. Her work has direct clinical and educational applications, and she regularly contributes to professional training for speech-language pathologists and teachers.
