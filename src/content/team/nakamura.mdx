Kaito Nakamura is a postdoctoral researcher in the Neural Computation Laboratory. His research focuses on the dynamical mechanisms underlying working memory in prefrontal cortex, combining multi-electrode array recordings with dimensionality reduction and RNN modeling.

He received his PhD from Kyoto University (2021) under the supervision of Prof. Hiroshi Tanaka, where he studied attractor dynamics in recurrent networks. Prior to his PhD, he studied Applied Mathematics at the University of Tokyo.

Outside the lab, Kaito is an avid jazz pianist and amateur astronomer.
