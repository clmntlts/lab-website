We are thrilled to announce that our paper *Riemannian geometry of neural population codes* has been published in **Nature Neuroscience** and highlighted as an Editor's Choice.

The work develops a geometric framework for analyzing neural manifolds that respects their intrinsic curvature, and shows that this curvature predicts behavioral performance across tasks. Code and data are openly available on GitHub.

Congratulations to all authors — Kaito, Aisha, and our collaborator John Cunningham!
