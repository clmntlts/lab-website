We are actively recruiting for the following positions, funded by our ERC Starting Grant:

**2 × PhD students** (fully funded, 3 years + 1 year extension)
- Strong background in neuroscience, physics, mathematics, or computer science
- Interest in bridging theory and experiment
- Starting September 2024

**1 × Postdoctoral Researcher** (2 years + 1 year extension)  
- PhD in computational neuroscience, machine learning, or related field
- Experience with large-scale neural data analysis

We strongly encourage applications from candidates of all backgrounds. The lab is committed to equity, diversity, and inclusion in science.

To apply, please send a CV and brief cover letter to elena.vasquez@university.edu with the subject line **Application 2024**.
