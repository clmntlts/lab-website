We are delighted to announce that Elena has been awarded an **ERC Starting Grant** of €1.5M for the project *Neural Geometry of Sensory Representations* (2023–2028).

This prestigious grant will fund a major expansion of our research into the geometric structure of neural representations across sensory hierarchies. We will be recruiting two PhD students and a postdoc — watch this space for open positions!
