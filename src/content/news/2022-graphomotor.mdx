date: 2022-06-01

category: Publication
featured: true
---

Coline Gosse published two papers this year examining graphomotor dysfunction in children with dyslexia. The first, in *Cortex*, reports behavioural evidence of handwriting movement difficulties; the second, in *Brain Sciences*, identifies altered resting-state functional connectivity in the writing network using fMRI.
